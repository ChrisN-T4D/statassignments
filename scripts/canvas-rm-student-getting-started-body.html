What is Methods Market?

Methods Market is where you read Pressbooks chapters, do Concept Review quizzes, and get Assignment Help for this capstone. Canvas holds your syllabus, due dates, grades, group work, worksheets, and IRB submissions — complete capstone assignments in Canvas after you have done the reading and practice in Methods Market.

Open Research Methods course home (Methods Market)

One-time setup (do this first)

You only need to do these steps once at the start of the semester.

Step 1 — Create your account

  1. Go to methods-market.clneu.com/auth.
  2. Click Create Account (or sign in if you already have one).
  3. Use an email you check regularly and a password you will remember (at least 6 characters).

Step 2 — Link your student key

Your instructor will give you a student key (example: 2026FA-X7K9M2). This links your account to the class roster so your practice is tracked without putting your name on analytics exports.

  1. While signed in, go to methods-market.clneu.com/claim.
  2. Select semester: Fall 2026 (or the active semester your instructor names).
  3. Enter your student key exactly as provided.
  4. Click Link My Profile.

Don't have a key yet? Ask your instructor — keys are usually sent by Canvas message or email in the first week.

Step 3 — Bookmark these links

  • Research Methods — course home
  • Assignment help (match Canvas assignments)
  • Textbook — Research Methods in Psychology (4th ed., Pressbooks)

Each week — reading, practice, and capstone work

  1. Check Canvas for this week's capstone deadlines (literature review, worksheets, IRB, etc.).
  2. Open Methods Market and select the Pressbooks chapter assigned for the week (see syllabus or instructor announcement).
  3. On the Topics tab, read the chapter (opens Pressbooks).
  4. On the Concept Review tab, complete the quiz for that chapter.
  5. Return to Canvas to work on phase assignments (drafts, worksheets, training, IRB).
  6. Use Assignment Help when you need tips for a specific Canvas assignment.

Note: Canvas does not automatically know when you finish Concept Review in Methods Market. Capstone grades come from what you submit in Canvas.

Troubleshooting

My progress isn't saved
Sign in at /auth and complete /claim with your student key.
Which chapter should I read this week?
Follow the syllabus weekly schedule in Canvas — Methods Market lists all chapters; Canvas tells you which one is due.
Stuck on a capstone assignment
Open Assignment Help, find your Canvas assignment by name, and use the linked chapters and tips.
Due dates and points
Always follow Canvas for deadlines and grading.
